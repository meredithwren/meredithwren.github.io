Where I'm From

Before I came to Brooklyn, I was living in Puerto Rico, which is home to many satos like me. My mom doesn't know much about what my life was like before she adopted me, but it was probably pretty lonely. Then the National Foster Alliance picked me up and and sent me to the United States to live in the big city!
